Daily Diet Plan for students <> (Adarsh,Shubhi)

Breakfast:

Option 1:

Veggie Omelette: Whisked eggs with chopped green vegetables (spinach, bell peppers, onions) cooked in a non-stick pan with a drizzle of olive oil.

A serving of plain yogurt topped with chopped walnuts and fresh fruits (apple, banana, grapes).

Option 2:

Overnight Chia Seed Pudding: Chia seeds soaked in milk with a dash of honey and topped with sliced almonds and anjeer (figs).

A glass of buttermilk or freshly squeezed orange juice.

Mid-Morning Snack:

Option 1:

Fresh Fruit Bowl: A mix of seasonal fruits (anar, orange, grapes) with a sprinkle of flex seeds.

Option 2:

Handful of Roasted Walnuts and Almonds.

Lunch:

Option 1:

Chicken Biryani: Fragrant basmati rice cooked with chicken and spices.

A side of cucumber and tomato salad with lemon dressing.

Option 2:

Dal with Rice: Lentil curry (dal) served with steamed rice.

A serving of mixed green vegetables stir-fried with minimal oil.

Afternoon Snack:

Option 1:

Fresh Fruit Chaat: Chopped apple, banana, and pomegranate seeds tossed with chaat masala and lemon juice.

Option 2:

Mutton Kebabs: Minced mutton kebabs seasoned with spices and grilled or cooked on a non-stick pan.

Dinner:

Option 1:

Grilled Fish: Fatty fish (e.g., salmon) marinated with herbs and grilled to perfection.

A side of steamed green vegetables (broccoli, beans) drizzled with a little olive oil.

Option 2:

Paneer (Cottage Cheese) and Vegetable Stir-Fry: Cubes of paneer cooked with mixed vegetables (bell peppers, onions) and flavored with spices.

A serving of plain yogurt or buttermilk.

Bedtime Snack:

Option 1:

Sliced Anjeer (Figs) and a glass of milk.

Option 2:

Soaked Monacca seeds in water (optional).
